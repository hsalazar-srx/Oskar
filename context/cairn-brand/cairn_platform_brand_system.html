Taglines
Primary — all audiences
Every stone in its place.
Short enough to live on a login screen. Implies intentionality, structure, and the idea that nothing is ad hoc — every part, every change, every approval has a defined position in the workflow.
Engineering audience
Change it once. Know it everywhere.
Speaks directly to the ECN pain point — today a change ripples through email threads, spreadsheets, and a legacy portal that cannot be extended. Cairn makes one approved change propagate to every affected BOM with full audit trail and ISO 13485-compliant traceability.
Procurement / supply chain
Know what you need before you need it.
Live pricing and availability from DigiKey, Mouser, Arrow, and Element14 — cached, normalised, and enriched against the M3 part master. Not just price lookup: EOL risk, alternate sourcing, and BOM impact before a PO is raised.
Leadership / Dream Factory framing
The engineering pillar of Dream Factory.
Cairn is not a replacement tool. It is the engineering workflow and intelligence pillar of the Scanfil APAC Dream Factory 2026 programme — and the platform foundation for MES integration, Data Warehouse, EDI/RPA, and predictive quality initiatives that follow.
Modules — the stones of Cairn
Ledger
Bill of Materials
Multi-level BOM management with M3 part master sync (MPDHED, MPDMAT), revision history, and live pricing enrichment. The version-controlled record of what gets built.
Shift
Engineering Change Notes
13-status ECN state machine with 16 configurable approval roles. Human-in-the-loop — no Movex write without explicit approval. BOM impact propagates automatically on close. Replaces Stargile.
Vein
Supplier & Component Data
Async multi-distributor API fan-out — DigiKey, Mouser, Arrow, Element14 — with Redis cache and per-supplier circuit breaker. Normalised pricing and availability that feeds Ledger and Shift. Replaces PLMServer APIManager entirely.
Trace
Audit & Lineage
Immutable SHA-256 hash chain across every ECN state transition, BOM revision, and supplier record change. ISO 13485-compliant lineage. Queryable by part, ECN, or date range. Audit-ready export.
Signal
Alerts & Risk Intelligence
EOL alerts, price spike detection, PCN notifications, and supply disruption signals — surfaced before they become procurement emergencies. AI-assisted alternate part suggestions mapped to the approved supplier list.
Core
M3 Integration & Part Master
The foundation stone. Bidirectional Movex sync (MITMAS, MIPUR, MPDHED, MPDMAT). IPN → MPN mapping, approved supplier lists, and standard cost write-back from market data. Movex is always the source of truth — Core enforces it.

Voice & tone
What Cairn sounds like
"3 components in this BOM are approaching end-of-life. Signal has identified 2 qualified alternates for each. Review before your next build cycle."
What Cairn does not sound like
"Warning: Potential supply chain disruption detected! Immediate action required for optimal procurement outcomes across your component portfolio."
On ECN approval
"ECN-0042 approved at Status 50. 7 BOMs updated in Ledger. Movex push queued — awaiting Document Controller confirmation before commit."
On an empty state
"No parts flagged for review. Cairn is watching."
On a Movex write
"Movex updated. PDS002MI AddComponent confirmed for 4 parts. Trace has the full audit record."
On a compliance query
"ECN-0031 — initiated by Bryan, approved by Engineering Manager and Document Controller, committed to Movex on 2026-03-14. Full SHA-256 chain available in Trace."
